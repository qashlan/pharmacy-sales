## ğŸ’¡ Common Questions

### Q: Does this include refunds?
**A:** No! All calculations automatically exclude refunds, showing only actual sales.

### Q: Can I compare non-consecutive months?
**A:** Yes! Compare ANY two months (e.g., Jan 2024 vs Oct 2025).

### Q: Can I export the data?
**A:** Yes! Download buttons in Tab 2 and Tab 3 export to CSV.

### Q: How far back can I see?
**A:** All months in your data file. The test shows 23 months of data available.

### Q: What if a category didn't exist in one month?
**A:** The comparison shows $0 for that month and calculates the change.
